## 23. Why AB-EXIT is Unstoppable: Ballot Initiative and 75% Support

### 23.1. Ballot Initiative Bypasses the Entire Elite

The ballot initiative in California (and other states with direct democracy): step 1 — collect 10% of signatures of registered voters (Stockton: ~20,000). Permission from the mayor, council, party, lawyer, NO ONE is required. Signatures are collected by citizens on the streets with a pen on paper. Step 2 — the County Clerk is OBLIGATED to verify the signatures (not the content, but the authenticity). The signatures are real, the signers are registered — the referendum is scheduled by law. The mayor cannot block it. Step 3 — the vote, a simple majority = adopted, it becomes part of the city charter. The mayor is obligated to execute it.

Appeal → Supreme Court → 6-3 for AB-EXIT (the conservative majority will support it as individual liberty).

**Counter-referendum.** One must collect another 20,000 signatures and convince people to vote for the REPEAL of the $780. Campaign: "Vote to take away your $780!" No one in history has ever won a campaign on "we will take your money." Probability: ~0%.

**Changing the Ballot Initiative Law.** The state legislature bans initiatives on the topic of electoral payments. In California = political suicide: Californians love ballot initiatives, Prop 13 (1978) = sacred. Probability: ~1%.

Total probability of full blocking: less than 5%. And even if blocked in one city — there are 19,502 municipalities in the US. Blocking all = impossible. One is enough.
