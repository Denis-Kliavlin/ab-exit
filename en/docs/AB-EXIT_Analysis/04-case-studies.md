## 57. The Korean Miracle Without Park Chung-hee: AB-EXIT as a Democratic Path to Consistency

In 1962, South Korea’s GDP per capita was $87 — less than Ghana's. The country had no steel industry, almost no paved roads, and the economy was built on agriculture and US aid.
