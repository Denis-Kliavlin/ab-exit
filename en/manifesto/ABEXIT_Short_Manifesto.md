# Your voice is worth money. Yet everyone receives the money except you.

You abstain from elections. For free. Voluntarily.

Do you know who profits from this?

**Parties** receive funds from the state budget for every vote. Your vote is missing—yet you still pay taxes.

**Television networks** receive millions for election advertising. You watch it—but you do not decide.

**Politicians** allocate the budget toward those who vote. For you—nothing. You are not a threat. You can be ignored.

Your voice generates revenue for everyone. Except you. For you, it is worth zero.

---

## Two buttons. Before every election.

🟢 **"I Vote."** — Your vote now carries more weight because a segment of the population has departed. You exert real influence.

🔵 **"I Take (~$200)."** — You exit this circus. Officially, voluntarily, honestly. The **State** (not a party, not an oligarch, not an envelope) pays you via your card. Automatically. You are not selling your vote to someone. You are simply stating: "I do not care for this performance—pay me for leaving the theater."

Four years later—the choice repeats. If you disliked the outcome without you—return and vote.

---

## This is not vote-buying

Vote-buying occurs when you are paid for a specific candidate. "Vote for Ivanov—here is some buckwheat."

YUNO is the opposite. The **State** pays you to honestly state: "I am indifferent. I do not wish to participate in this comedy." No single candidate profits from this. You simply depart—with money instead of empty hands.

48% already abstain. For free. YUNO simply pays for what you are already doing.

---

## What you receive

**"I Take"** — you receive $200 from the **State** for what you previously did for free. All rights remain: protest, court, freedom of speech.

**"I Vote"** — your vote carries twice the weight. Half have departed, while you remained. For the first time, your single vote truly decides.

In both cases, you cease to be invisible.

---

## Why this is withheld from you

Because currently, you are a free resource. You remain silent for free; the authorities ignore you for free. Everyone is satisfied. Except you.

YUNO breaks this cycle. Either the **State** pays you—or your vote becomes a weapon. No incumbent politician will be the first to propose YUNO. It is to their advantage that you stay home for free.

---

**Why is abstaining for free considered normal, while exiting this comedy for $200 from the State is deemed a crime?**

Four AIs could not provide an answer. $10,000 awaits anyone who can.

**YUNO. Your voice. Your money. Your exit.**
