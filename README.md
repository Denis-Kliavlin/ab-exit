# AB-EXIT™

**A — I vote. B — I take $200. One exit.**

Before every election, every citizen gets two buttons:
- 🟢 **A — "I vote."** Your vote now weighs more, because others left. You actually matter.
- 🔵 **B — "I take ~$200."** You exit. Officially, voluntarily, honestly. The **state** (not a party, not an oligarch) pays you. Automatically. Every 4 years — choose again.

## The $10,000 Question

> *"If 48% already don't vote — for free — and the state already pays parties for votes they receive, why is it a crime to pay a citizen for honestly saying 'I don't want to participate' — when all their other rights remain intact?"*

## Proof of Origin

This concept was first documented on **July 14, 2014, 04:11** in a Google Doc by Denis Klyavlin (Chișinău, Moldova):

> "Голос можно продать. Или точнее аннулировать за деньги. Если я не хочу голосовать то я могу за деньги заключить договор о том что я АННИГИЛИРУЮ свой голос и обязуюсь не голосовать на выборах. Мне дают за это деньги."

Translation: *"A vote can be sold. Or more precisely, annulled for money. If I don't want to vote, I can sign a contract to ANNIHILATE my vote and commit to not voting in elections. I get paid for this."*

Twelve years before five AI systems confirmed: he was right.
